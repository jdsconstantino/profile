Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DeskXP (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website deskxp.com or interact with our services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data in compliance with applicable laws, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Philippines), GDPR (EU), and CCPA (California).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services or communicate with you, including:

  • Personal details you provide — such as name, email address, company, or phone number.
  • Information submitted through forms (e.g., contact or “Personalized Specs” requests).
  • Technical delivery logs from our hosting or font CDN (e.g., basic request metadata used for service reliability and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • Prepare tailored proposals and specifications based on your inputs.
  • Improve site reliability and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations or enforce agreements.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under one or more of the following bases:

  • Consent – when you submit a form or request outreach.
  • Legitimate Interest – to operate and improve our services in a way you would reasonably expect.
  • Legal Obligation – when processing is necessary to comply with applicable laws.

4. Cookies

We do not set or use tracking cookies. Our site does not deploy analytics, advertising pixels, or other trackers. If we ever add optional analytics in the future, we will present a clear consent banner and allow you to opt in or decline before any tracking begins.

For a friendly summary, see our Cookie Fun page — it’s the short version that simply says we don’t collect cookies.

Your browser or third-party services (like your ISP or corporate firewall) may still add their own headers or caching behavior — that’s outside our control and not used by DeskXP to track you.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted providers to deliver and secure content:

  • GitHub Pages – website hosting and delivery.
  • Google Fonts – font delivery via secure CDN.
  • Email / Cloud Providers – communication and file storage when you contact us.
  • Namecheap - domain registration services
  • Cloudflare - DNS, security and performance services

These services may process limited technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and device information) in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell or rent any personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact and project information only as long as necessary for business or legal purposes. By default, we keep form submissions for up to 12 months, unless you request earlier deletion.

7. Data Security

We use SSL/TLS, access controls, and limited storage practices to safeguard your information. No method is 100% secure, but we continually review measures to protect against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your country. When that occurs, we take steps to ensure an adequate level of protection consistent with applicable law.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent.
  • Complain to your local data protection authority if you believe your rights are violated.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@deskxp.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted here with the revised date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@deskxp.com.
