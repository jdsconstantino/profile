From Scattered Answers to an AI-Aware Help Center

High-consideration eCommerce means lots of pre-purchase questions and WISMO churn. Support lived across email threads, CMS notes, and tribal memory — agents rewrote the same answers and customers waited for basics.

↓ Vol

01

Use Cases

Centralize product guidance (sizes, handing, glass options, lead times), tame WISMO volume, and make the AI agent trustworthy. The goal: one place customers and agents both rely on — structured content the bot can cite, and macros that stop ad-hoc replies.

Before
  • Product knowledge scattered across email, CMS, and memory
  • Agents rewriting the same answers from scratch
  • AI bot hallucinating answers with no source content
  • No structured contact form — endless back-and-forth
  • No visibility into what customers searched before tickets
After
  • Structured help center: Plan → Choose → Order → Track → Install
  • Macros link to canonical articles — no more rewrites
  • AI bot grounded on help center, quotes with source links
  • Form fields require order #, door series, reason code
  • Explore views track search-to-ticket and deflection by intent
02

Our Approach

We designed a help center that mirrors how shoppers decide: Plan → Choose → Order → Track → Install → Warranty. Each category used strict templates (Purpose, When to use, Steps, Specs, Edge cases, Links) so answers stay consistent regardless of who wrote the article.

Articles were tagged by intent (pre-purchase, order status, installation) and mapped to macros, so agents reply once with a canonical link instead of rewrites. The AI agent was grounded on this corpus and instructed to quote articles verbatim with links, not hallucinate.

"One knowledge spine, clean macros, AI that answers from source — not vibes."

We added form-based contact with required fields (order #, door series, glass family) to cut back-and-forth, and Explore views to track search-to-ticket conversion and deflection by intent.

03

Success Story

Repeat "one-touch" questions (WISMO, sizing, handing) dropped materially once the AI agent was restricted to the help center and agents linked articles instead of rewriting. First-response quality improved consistently.

Leadership finally saw what customers searched before contacting support — which guided the next wave of articles and reduced ticket creation at the source.

eCommerce · Europe · Zendesk Support Plan

Scaling CX on a Budget Plan

Sales spiked; tickets, complaints, and disputes followed. On a Support-tier plan with no enterprise add-ons, we had to ship scale with constraints — automate the boring, surface the risky, and give leadership a live view without buying new tools.

Shopify → Zendesk Dispute Reduction Automation & Macros Multilingual Dashboards Governance
~30%
Net reduction in total disputes
Live
Looker Studio dashboards — no new spend
Multi
Language macro variants auto-applied by locale
01

Use Cases

Stabilize CX during a demand surge: cut one-touch noise, prevent avoidable chargebacks, and shorten the dispute cycle. Give agents canonical answers in multiple languages, enforce SLAs, and give leadership a real-time dashboard — all without upgrading the plan.

Before
  • Disputes rising with no early-warning system
  • Agents writing different answers in every language
  • No Shopify order context inside tickets
  • No dashboard — leadership flying blind on KPIs
  • Chargebacks escalating before CX could intervene
After
  • Pre-dispute tickets auto-created on risk signals
  • Macro library with translation variants per locale
  • Shopify orders, payment status, RMA flags in every ticket
  • Looker Studio dashboard for FRT, CSAT, disputes by gateway
  • Disputes SOP with evidence packs and timeline by processor
02

Our Approach

Wire the context in: used Shopify APIs and available Zendesk endpoints to inject orders, payment status, fulfillment events, and RMA flags directly into tickets. Built a lightweight middleware to proactively create tickets on risk signals (failed capture, carrier exception, refund request keywords) so we control the narrative before it becomes a chargeback.

Make answers instant, in any language: codified macros per intent (WISMO, sizing, returns, refund timing) with translation variants. Triggers apply locale based on Shopify shipping country and auto-tag by intent for reporting.

"Connect the data, pre-empt the problems, standardize the reply, measure the fallout — all on a Support plan."

Dashboards without new spend: Apps Script → incremental ticket sync to Sheets → Looker Studio. Views for FRT/AHT/CSAT, refund rate by intent, pre-dispute outreach, and disputes by gateway/carrier. Scheduled snapshots for month-end.

03

Success Story

Total disputes trended down by ~25–35% (net ≈ −30%) as pre-dispute outreach and clean evidence packs kicked in. KPIs became visible daily via Looker Studio — agents spent less time copy-pasting thanks to macros, triggers, automations, and translation variants.

Leadership finally saw where disputes were born (intent → gateway → carrier), and we tuned content to deflect more of them at the source.
