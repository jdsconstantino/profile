About DeskXP

DeskXP is a CX consultancy that closes the gap between your business and your customers. We deliver end-to-end support operations—from building teams to shipping systems—covering agent staffing, workflow design, ticketing/CRM implementation, Help Center & knowledge, QA and training programs, SLAs and routing, automation & AI assistants, analytics, and continuous improvement. Outcome: faster response, lower cost-per-resolution, and a support experience customers actually trust.
